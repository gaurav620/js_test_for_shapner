## Category 3: Behavioral (STAR Method)

### Q7: Tell me about a time you handled pressure

**Answer**:

- **S**: Final semester ‚Äî project submission, patent filing, and IBM SkillsBuild certification all due in the same month.
- **T**: I needed to deliver all three without compromising quality.
- **A**: Created a priority matrix, dedicated mornings to the project, afternoons to patent documentation, and evenings to certification. Communicated clearly with my team about deadlines.
- **R**: All three delivered on time ‚Äî project graded A, patent published, and certification earned.

### Q8: Describe a time you worked in a team

**Answer**:

- **S**: Bike Rental project, 3-member team.
- **T**: Deliver MVP in 4 weeks.
- **A**: I took the backend (API + database), assigned frontend to one member, and testing to another. We did daily 15-min standups.
- **R**: MVP delivered on time, selected for department tech showcase. The clear role division was key.

### Q9: Tell me about a time you failed and what you learned

**Answer**:
"In my first project, I tried to build everything without Git version control. A bad code change broke my entire app and I had no way to revert. I lost 2 days of work. Since then, I **commit frequently** with meaningful messages, use branches for features, and never work directly on `main`. It taught me that good habits save more time than they cost."
